Greek Baklava

Source: Greek Baklava from Allrecipes

Rate This Recipe

What skill level is the recipe? Easy Intermediate Hard

Ingredients (no measurements provided)

  • Phyllo Dough
  • Chopped walnuts and/or pistachios
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla
  • Honey

Directions

  1. Layer Phyllo with Nuts - Stack eight sheets of phyllo dough, brushing with melted butter between each layer, to create a sturdy base. Sprinkle it with a mixture of chopped nuts and cinnamon. Top with two sheets of phyllo dough and follow with the nut mixture. Repeat this process a few more times, until the nut mixture is gone. Top with about eight more layers of phyllo, buttering each layer.
  2. Cut and Bake - Cut the baklava into diamond or square shapes. Be sure to cut all the way to the bottom of the pan. Bake until golden and crisp.
  3. Finish With Syrup - Boil water and sugar until sugar is melted. Add vanilla and honey. Remove the baklava from the oven and immediately pour syrup over it. Let cool before serving.
